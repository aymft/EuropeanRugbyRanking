How the ranking works

The ranking is based on an Elo rating system adapted to European club rugby. Each club has a numerical rating, which is updated after every match. Beating a highly rated opponent increases a club's rating more than beating a lower-rated opponent, while losing to a lower-rated team has a stronger negative impact.

The model also accounts for home advantage, score margin and competition type. European fixtures can therefore have a different weight from domestic league matches, reflecting their cross-league importance.

The arrows in the table show how many positions a club has gained or lost compared to the previous ranking. The Elo difference shows the point variation between the previous rating and the current computed rating.
